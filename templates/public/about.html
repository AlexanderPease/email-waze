What is NTWRK?
NTWRK is a relationship tool. It works on three levels:
  1. Helps you stay in touch with important contacts by setting reminders.
  2. Share contacts with your colleagues and friends.
  3. Find people and companies by searching our global database.



What is a "NTWRK?"
A “NTWRK” is our word for “team.” Users in a NTWRK share access to their contacts. NTWRKs also tracks how strongly users are connected to the people and companies in their address book.

You can be in multiple NTWRKs. For example, a NTWRK with your company, a NTWRK with your best friends, or a NTWRK for all your board members. Think of it as a more useful version of connecting on LinkedIn.


What are reminders?
Reminders are a simple way to stay in touch with your most valued contacts. For example, you can set a reminder to email Steve at least once a month. NTWRK will automatically check your inbox and let you know if you ever go more than a month without talking to Steve.


What is signal?
NTWRK automatically calculates how strongly people are connected to one another. Search results will show you how you are connected to someone via your NTWRKs, and how strong that connection.
