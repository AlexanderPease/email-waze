What is NTWRK?
NTWRK is a relationship tool. It works on three levels: 1) let’s you manage your own contacts by creating reminders, 2) share contacts with your colleagues and friends, and 3) search a global database of people and companies to email.
What is a "NTWRK?"
A “NTWRK” is our word for “team.” Users in a NTWRK share access to their contacts. NTWRKs also track how strongly users are connected to the people and companies in their address book.

You can be in multiple NTWRKs. For example, a NTWRK with your company, a NTWRK with your best friends, or a NTWRK for all your board members. Think of it as a more useful version of connecting on LinkedIn.
What are reminders?
Reminders are a simple way to stay in touch with your most valued contacts. For example, you can set a reminder to email Steve at Facebook at least once a month. Based on your emails, NTWRK will automatically let you know if you ever go more than a month without talking to Steve. {% end %}
